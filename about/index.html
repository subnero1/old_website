About

Subnero provides innovative solutions to facilitate underwater wireless networked communications. These solutions include a state-of-the-art software defined underwater modem and a highly customisable network stack that adapts to a variety of environments and applications.

Subnero's Water Assessment Network (SWAN) uses autonomous robots to monitor the quality of water in reservoirs and lakes.

Mission

Offer high performance underwater Communications, Navigation, Monitoring & Sensing solutions for commercial deployments.

Vision

To be the de facto standard for underwater communication & sensing networks.
