About

Subnero provides innovative solutions to facilitate underwater wireless networked communications.

The solutions not only include a state-of-the-art software-defined underwater modem, but also a highly customizable network stack, UnetStack, that adapts to a variety of environments and applications.

Mission

Offer high performance underwater Communications, Navigation, Monitoring & Sensing solutions for commercial deployments.

Vision

To be the de facto standard for underwater communication & sensing networks.
