By Train

Kent Ridge station in the circle line is the nearest MRT station to our office and is located at National University Campus. Once you get down at the Kent Ridge station, take the exit towards Lower Kent Ridge Road (exit A) to get to the Kent Ridge Station bus stop (Stop ID: 18331). Take SBS bus 95 towards Kent Ridge Terrace (or internal shuttle service A1).
Get down at "Yusof Ishak House" bus stop (Stop ID: 16171) and walk towards the NUS Visitors Lodge. We are located at level 2 of block E4, which is the building behind the Visitors Lodge. By Bus • Take SBS bus 95 or 151 towards Kent Ridge Terrace and get down at "Yusof Ishak House" bus stop (Stop ID: 16171) • Take SBS bus 96 (Clementi Loop) and get down at "Opposite Yusof Ishak House" bus stop (Stop ID: 16179) • Alternatively, you can take any of the NUS internal shuttle buses which goes to the above bus stops. After getting down, walk towards the NUS Visitors Lodge. We are located at level 2 of block E4, which is the building behind the Visitors Lodge.
By taxi Take a taxi from anywhere in Singapore, instruct the driver to go to National University of Singapore and drop you near the Visitors Lodge near Yusof Ishak House. We are located at level 2 of block E4, which is the building behind the Visitors Lodge.
