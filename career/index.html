Sales & Business Development Manager

You will be a member of a team with global ambitions. Our solutions and business model are designed to cater to the variances of the industries we address. This role will require you to evangelize the benefits of our solutions to a variety of customer organizations within the subsea / marine engineering, environmental monitoring, defense as well as oil & gas industries.

Roles & Responsibilities:

You would educate and develop the assigned territory, building the sales pipeline by tapping into your relationships in those markets. You will be expected to develop and manage relationships with the customers, advocating Subnero’s offerings, with the goal of ultimately generating viable commercial contracts for Subnero.

What we expect from you:

A self-driven, enthusiastic and passionate individual; possess excellent communication skills and have a proven track record in growing business. You have an analytical approach to sales and are able to work on a consistent basis towards closing major sales opportunities across geographies and ensuring customer delight.

  • BA/BS degree or equivalent practical experience.
  • Relevant work experience in Sub-sea / Marine / Oil & Gas / Defence / Environmental (atleast one is essential; additional domains a plus).
  • Execute quickly with limited guidance on cross-functional projects; anticipate changes in direction and scope.
  • Persuasive communication skills – Be able to explain the benefits Subnero brings to bear to all layers within the customer organization.
  • You would also identify and implement suitable partnerships as may be required for sales, distribution & technology purposes.
  • Your focus would initially be on the Asia-Pacific region, though you may also be assigned as the first point of business contact for our partners and prospective clients across the globe.
  • This role can be a great step for your career and your chance to get in on the ground floor of a company with global reach and the potential to make a genuine difference to the world.
  • Keen attention to detail; proven problem resolution skills.
  • Ability to work independently on multiple large, individual projects or major segments of complex company-wide projects.
  • Ability to generate options and create a positive working experience.
  • Comfortable working in a startup environment; “Can-do” attitude a must.

Quality Assurance Engineer / Mechanical Engineer

Our engineering team designs and builds the hardware, software and networking technologies that power all of our customers’ services, operating in demanding usage scenarios. Subnero is looking for a quality assurance engineer to join this growing team in Singapore.

Roles & Responsibilities:

Executes planned and systematic activities implemented in a quality system so that quality requirements for a product or service will be fulfilled. Those activities include:

  • Develop the processes and procedures to make sure the products manufactured or the service delivered will be of good quality.
  • Define the standards/methodology to be followed in order to meet the customer requirements.
  • Monitor the development process throughout its entire cycle (e.g., performing audits and reviews) comparing the results with standards.
  • Monitoring the activities and ensuring conformance to the standard ISO 9001 or CMMI.
  • Liaise with third party vendors for various product conformance tests (e.g. Hydrostatic pressure, shock, environmental etc.)

What we expect from you:

  • A passion for developing well-engineered electronics systems.
  • A degree or diploma in Mechanical Engineering, or equivalent.
  • Experience in dealing with product quality assurance procedure.
  • Experience with various industry standard EQTs.
  • 2+ years of work experience in a related role.
